About

Hi I'm Amrit Parashar. I'm a professional photographer with a passion for landscape photography. I'm so lucky my job is to travel around the world and capture its beauty. Some of the most beautiful places I’ve been include Santorini (Greece), Amalfi Coast (Italy), Lofoten Islands (Norway), Iguazú Falls (Argentina), Bagan (Myanmar), and Mù Cang Chải (Vietnam).

Check out some of my most recent blog posts below and stay tuned for my latest post on my visit to Boracay (Philippines), coming soon!
